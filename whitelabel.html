What is white-label?

How white-label development works

White-label web development is a business model where SARVAYA builds websites, apps, and digital products that agencies rebrand and deliver to their own clients. The agency owns the client relationship and presents the work under its own brand. SARVAYA operates entirely behind the scenes under NDA, providing the technical execution while the agency captures the revenue. This model lets agencies expand service offerings, take on larger projects, and scale capacity without hiring additional in-house staff. View our portfolio →
