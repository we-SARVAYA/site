About NextGen PMs

A community for aspiring, intern, and early-career PMs.
Insights, internships, and conversations that matter.

NextGen PMs is a growing community built for aspiring Product Managers, PM interns, early-career professionals, and those transitioning into Product Management.

We live across two spaces:

  • 💬 Our active WhatsApp community, where members share opportunities and advice in real-time

  • 🌐 This website, where everything is organized — resources, internships, articles, and more

Whether you're just starting out or looking to sharpen your PM skills, this is the place for you.

Who Can Join?


✅ Aspiring Product Managers
✅ PM Interns
✅ Early-career Product Managers
✅ People transitioning from other fields into PM
✅ Students or recent grads exploring the PM path

Why Join Us?


🔹 Access curated, high-quality PM resources
🔹 Find internships and early-career job listings
🔹 Learn from PMs already in the field
🔹 Share your knowledge and get recognized
🔹 Be part of a focused, encouraging peer group
🔹 Use our structured website and real-time WhatsApp group together for the best experience

Community Rules


🚫 No spam or unrelated content
💬 Keep all discussions focused on Product Management
🤝 Be respectful, helpful, and open to learning
📤 Share useful, actionable resources or insights

Together, we’re building a space that empowers the next generation of Product Managers 🚀

Upcoming Feature: AI Agent for PMs


We're excited to share that we're working on an AI-powered Assistant built specifically for Product Managers!
This AI agent will help you with:

🔹 Generate user stories, PRDs, and roadmaps
🔹 Summarize articles, videos, and key PM content
🔹 Analyze user feedback and product data
🔹 Get tool suggestions and curated resources
🔹 Save time and focus on solving real problems

Our goal is to make product management smarter, faster, and easier — especially for those starting out.

Stay tuned. The future of PM is getting an upgrade. 🚀
