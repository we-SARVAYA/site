What we automate

If a human is doing it repeatedly, an AI agent can probably do it cheaper.

SARVAYA builds AI automations that run quietly in the background, doing the work that drains your team's hours. We start with the workflow, not the technology - mapping the actual sequence of steps a human takes, then replacing or augmenting them with a combination of LLMs, retrieval, scripted logic, and human-in-the-loop approvals where needed. The output is a system that runs on its own, gets measured against real metrics (hours saved, response quality, cost per task), and pays for itself within weeks. We've shipped automations for sales, ops, content, support, and internal knowledge work - across teams of 3 to 300. Every engagement runs under NDA.

Process

How we ship an automation

01

Map the workflow

30-minute call to map the exact sequence of human steps, inputs, and outputs. We write down what's slow, what's repetitive, and what's worth automating first.

02

Pick the stack

Models (Claude, GPT-5, Gemini), orchestration (n8n, Make, custom Node), retrieval, integrations. We pick for cost, latency, accuracy - not hype.

03

Build the pilot

One scoped automation built end-to-end in 1-3 weeks. You review on real data, we tune prompts, eval, and edge cases until it's actually production-ready.

04

Deploy & monitor

Live in your infra with audit logs, cost tracking, error alerts, and a simple dashboard so non-technical team members can see exactly what's happening.

05

Measure & expand

30-day ROI review. We confirm hours saved, quality, cost per task - then either hand off, retain, or scope the next automation in your backlog.
