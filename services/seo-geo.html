Rank in Google. Get cited by AI. Both, in one engagement.

Search is splitting in two: classic Google results, and AI-powered answers from Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Bing Copilot. We optimize for both - technical, content, schema, and AI-citation signals - so your brand shows up wherever your buyers are actually looking.

Get Found Everywhere

SARVAYA combines SEO, AEO, and GEO into a single engagement so your brand ranks in Google AND gets cited by AI search engines.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) targets traditional Google rankings. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) wins featured snippets and direct answers. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) targets AI-powered answers from Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Bing Copilot. We deliver technical fixes, schema, original content, and citation monitoring under one roof — with monthly reporting on rankings, AI citations, and conversions.

What we do

Most "SEO" agencies haven't caught up to AI search. We have.

Search behavior has changed faster than the SEO industry. People still Google - but they also ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's own AI Overviews to summarize answers and recommend brands. If your content isn't structured to be cited by those engines, you're invisible to a growing slice of your buyers. SARVAYA runs SEO, AEO (Answer Engine Optimization), and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) as one combined practice. We do the technical work (Core Web Vitals, schema, crawl, indexation), the content work (passages structured for citation, comparisons, FAQs, hub-and-spoke clusters), and the AI-search work (llms.txt, brand entity signals, citation monitoring) in a single engagement so nothing falls between the cracks. Curious Minds

SEO, AEO & GEO FAQs

SEO targets traditional Google rankings. AEO targets featured snippets, People Also Ask, and direct answers. GEO targets AI-powered search experiences - Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT search, Perplexity, Bing Copilot - where your content is cited inside generated answers. We do all three because they share the same technical foundation but require different content patterns.

Technical fixes can show within 2-4 weeks. Content rankings typically start moving in 8-12 weeks. AI-search citations (AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity) often appear faster than traditional rankings - sometimes within 4-6 weeks of publishing well-structured, citable content.

No serious agency can guarantee specific positions - search algorithms change weekly and any agency claiming guarantees is lying or about to use tactics that get you penalized. We guarantee process, transparency, and measurable progress against a baseline you set.

We make sure AI crawlers can access your content (robots.txt, llms.txt), structure passages so they're directly citable, add comprehensive Schema.org markup, build entity-level brand mentions across the open web, and write content that answers questions in the format AI engines pull from. We track citations using SERP and AI-search monitoring tools.

Both. We can ship original content (blog posts, landing pages, comparisons, FAQs) tuned for SEO + AEO + GEO, or we can optimize and restructure your existing content. Most engagements include a mix of both.

Audits start at a fixed bracket. 90-day Sprints and ongoing Retainers are scoped per project after a discovery call. We send a transparent fixed quote with a clear deliverable list within 24-48 hours of the call.
